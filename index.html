I recently graduated from Penn, where I studied finance and computer science. I'm from New Albany, Ohio, and a Midwesterner at heart. I'm a practical idealist, and I care about furthering environmental sustainability and social equity through media, film, and tech. I’m finishing up three films on water scarcity, climate change, and their effects on gender and ageism in Bhutan, one of the world’s greenest nations. My co-filmmaker, Alina Peng, and I travelled to Bhutan this past January after we were awarded a National Geographic Early Career Grant. We’re trying to release the documentary shorts sometime later in 2020, so be on the lookout!
